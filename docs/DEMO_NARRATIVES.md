## 6. Objection Handling

### "How is this different from ChatGPT?"

> "ChatGPT has broad knowledge but no GS1 expertise and potentially outdated regulatory information. ISA is built on authoritative, versioned datasets specific to EU regulations and GS1 standards. Every answer cites its sources, and we track data freshness."

### "Can I trust AI-generated advice?"

> "ISA is designed for trust. Every answer includes citations to authoritative sources, confidence scores based on evidence strength, and data provenance. Our advisory content goes through Lane C governance review. ISA is a research accelerator, not a replacement for professional judgment."

### "What if the regulations change?"

> "ISA monitors 7 regulatory news sources and updates its knowledge base regularly. Our dataset registry tracks refresh schedules, and we flag when information may be outdated. You'll always know how current your information is."

### "Is this just for large companies?"

> "ISA is designed for GS1 NL members of all sizes. The Compliance Roadmap Generator adapts to company size (SME vs. large) and maturity level. Small companies benefit most from self-service access to expert-level guidance."
