## Core Intent Shift

ISA is now a **Proof of Concept (PoC)** focused on:

### What ISA IS:
- Demonstrating capabilities, integration patterns, technical feasibility
- Showcasing innovative AI techniques applied to ESG-GS1 mapping
- Proving value through feature quality and bidirectional integration
- Treating GS1 NL v3.1.34 as operational and sufficient

### What ISA is NOT:
- ‚ùå An MVP aiming at completeness or standard finalization
- ‚ùå A tool for producing/extending complete GS1 standards
- ‚ùå A comprehensive coverage platform (unless strictly necessary)
- ‚ùå A production-ready compliance system

---

## Development Focus Principles

### 1. Capabilities over Coverage
Improve and demonstrate **what ISA can do**, not **how much it covers**

### 2. Quality over Quantity
- Feature functionality
- Technical quality
- Performance
- Data pipelines suitable for demonstration use

### 3. Integration over Isolation
Prefer **fewer, deeply integrated capabilities** over many isolated ones

### 4. Innovation over Completion
Leverage **innovative AI techniques** and methodologies

### 5. Demonstration over Production
Build for **insight and capability demonstration**, not formal standard readiness

## Integration Mindset

Always ask:
1. How can this feature **consume outputs** from other ISA features?
2. How can this feature **produce outputs** useful to other ISA features?
3. What shared data, models, or reasoning can be leveraged?
