## Risks & Mitigation

### Identified Risks

1. **Vision Drift**
   - Risk: Feature requests that violate anti-goals
   - Mitigation: MANUS_EXECUTION_BRIEF is normative, deviations require explicit approval

2. **Dataset Licensing Ambiguity**
   - Risk: Unclear terms for GS1 or EU datasets
   - Mitigation: Access type and license documented in catalog, legal review pending

3. **Version Confusion**
   - Risk: Mixing current and future versions in outputs
   - Mitigation: Explicit version labeling in catalog, default to "current" in outputs

4. **Repository Bloat**
   - Risk: Accumulating non-advisory content over time
   - Mitigation: Archive policy established, cleanup log required
