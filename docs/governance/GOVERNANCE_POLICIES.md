## ISA Governance Policies

**Date:** 2026-02-05

### 1. Roadmap Review Board (RRB)

**Purpose:** To formalize the process for updating the `ISA_DEVELOPMENT_ROADMAP.md`.

**Composition:** The RRB consists of the Project Owner (Human) and the lead developers.

**Responsibilities:** The RRB convenes monthly to evaluate progress, set new priorities, and update the roadmap. All changes to the roadmap must be approved by the RRB.

### 2. ISA Core Team

**Purpose:** To clarify the role and responsibilities of the "ISA Core Team".

**Definition:** The "ISA Core Team" is defined as a group of senior developers and architects responsible for the technical direction and quality of the ISA project.

**Responsibilities:** The Core Team is responsible for reviewing pull requests, maintaining code quality, and mentoring other developers.

### 3. Semantic Versioning (SemVer)

**Purpose:** To formalize release and version management.

**Policy:** The project will follow SemVer 2.0.0 for versioning. Each release will be documented with a changelog and communicated to all stakeholders.
